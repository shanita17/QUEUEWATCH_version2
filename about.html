🇿🇦 OUR MISSION

Built for the
people of SA.

QueueWatch SA is a community-driven platform that gives citizens real-time visibility into queue conditions at public service offices — before they leave home.

// The Problem

Hours wasted. Every single day.

Millions of South Africans visit Home Affairs, SASSA offices, clinics, and licensing departments every year. With no way to check conditions before arriving, people take time off work, arrange transport, and travel long distances — only to find 3-hour queues or system downtime.

This uncertainty hits the most vulnerable people hardest. Those who can least afford to waste a day are the ones doing it most often.

3+ avg hours wasted
per visit
// Our Solution

Community-powered information.

QueueWatch SA lets people already at a branch share real-time updates with everyone else. No government integration needed — just citizens helping citizens.

Search for your nearest branch, see the latest wait time reports, and decide whether it's worth going today.

Live community reports
updated in real time
// How It Works
01

Search your branch

Find any Home Affairs, SASSA, clinic, or licensing office near you using our search and filter tools.

02

Check the queue

See real-time reports submitted by people currently at the branch — including wait times and service status.

03

Submit a report

Already there? Take 10 seconds to submit a report and help the next person plan their visit better.

04

Plan smarter

Use the daily average and peak times to choose the best time to visit and avoid the longest queues.
